Macy’s Friends and Family Sale Savings

A few times a year, Macy’s has an amazing Friends and Family sale where you can get up to 30% off your online purchase of almost anything. These sales are the perfect time to revamp your wardrobe, update your home decor, and get gifts for all occasions at exceptional prices.

Macy's Info & Tips

How can I find a Macy’s near me so that I can get free in-store pickup?

You can do it all right on www.macys.com! When you first enter the website, a nearby store will be displayed but it may not be the one you prefer. To find the closest location, click on the Stores link where you’ll see a map and listings of neighboring locations. Select a radius to expand or contract your options, or if you’re looking for a specific department you might want to filter using the criteria of your choice.

  • Watch for annual sales events or semi-annual sales events such as the popular Friends and Family event where you can save up to 25% off almost everything in the store.
  • Pop-up sales are a fast way to save. When you see one of these sales, make sure to get the deals while they last.
  • Visit the Deals section every time you shop to find coupons, offers, and all the latest and most up-to-date promotions and deals in one place.
  • Visit Savings.com every time you shop to find Macy’s online coupons you can use to add discounts to your order.
